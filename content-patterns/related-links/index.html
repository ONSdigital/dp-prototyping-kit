More about this topic

This section allows users who want to find out more about the topic of this bulletin to find more detailed or technical analysis.

Provide up to five links to:

  • articles containing deeper analysis of the topic, which cannot fit in the bulletin template
  • analysis of the latest data alongside other data sources
  • other releases which also reference this topic

Include the title of what you're linking to, a subtitle which includes the type of content you're linking to, along with a 30-word description of what the link points to.

For example

More about violent crime

The nature of violent crime in England and Wales: year ending March 2017
Article | Released on 19 July 2017
Long-term trends and the circumstances of violence (do not include the most recent statistics for the year ending December 2017).

ONS Focus on Violent Crime and Sexual Offences: Year ending March 2015
Article | Released on 19 July 2015
Analysis of violent crime, based on the Crime Survey for England and Wales and police recorded crime.

You may also be interested in

Use this section to link to content which:

  • is not specifically about the topic on the page
  • is not covered by another page in this release
  • may be of interest to users who have visited this page

Someone who is reading about employment data may also be interested in earnings, or the gender pay gap, for example. Someone reading about birth rates may be interested in baby names.

Include the title of what you're linking to, a subtitle which includes the type of content you're linking to, along with a 30-word description of what the link points to.
